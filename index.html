Taller Triangle


Our space is a 3D printing clay workshop and shared workspace in the heart of El Born, Barcelona. Built for designers, makers, and independent professionals, it offers the tools and technology for hands-on production, along with flexible space to work, think, and collaborate.

At the center is our fully equipped 3D printing clay workshop—ideal for prototyping and small-batch production with both traditional and digital tools. The connected shared workspace suits those who balance computer work with hands-on making, or simply want to work around others. A flexible showroom ties it all together, available for showcasing your work or hosting small events.

01 WORKSHOP 32M2

Built for hands-on making, the Workshop features a generous workbench for up to eight people. The space includes both Clay 3D printers and FDM 3D printers, a ceramic kiln, storage, and a sink. A large window opens up the room while still maintaining a private atmosphere. Additionally, a large door provides the option to showcase your workflow to the public.

02 CO- WORKING 30M2

Designed for collaborative work, the Co-Working Room offers a spacious table with dedicated workspaces for up to six people. A large door opens onto the lively streets of El Born, bringing in fresh air and vibrant energy. The room connects directly to both the Workshop and the Showroom, making it easy to move between spaces and collaborate

03 SHOWROOM 28M2

Set up for flexibility, the Showroom can be used for both gatherings and showcasing work. It features a kitchen counter and a large open floor area, allowing for a variety of setups. Shelves along some of the walls offer space to display projects and products. This adaptable room offers the opportunity to host small events, exhibitions, or pop-up stores.
